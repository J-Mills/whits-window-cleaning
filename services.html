Services & Pricing

Professional exterior cleaning for homes and businesses across Berkshire, Hampshire & Surrey. All work fully insured.

Window Cleaning

My window cleaning service covers both interior and exterior windows for residential and commercial properties. I use pure water-fed pole systems which deliver a completely streak-free finish without the need for ladders on most properties — safer for me, less disruption for you.

  • Residential & commercial
  • Interior & exterior
  • Purified water-fed pole system
  • Streak-free guaranteed

Fascia & Soffit Cleaning

Over time, fascias and soffits accumulate dirt, green algae, mould, and grime that can be difficult to remove with standard methods. My specialist cleaning process restores them to their original condition, improving your home's kerb appeal and preventing long-term deterioration.

  • All UPVC materials
  • Algae & mould removal
  • Improves kerb appeal
  • Protects from deterioration

Conservatory Cleaning

A full conservatory clean covering the roof panels, frames, gutters, and all glass surfaces inside and out. I use specialist equipment to safely access and clean the roof, removing moss, algae, and built-up grime for a like-new result.

  • Full roof & glass clean
  • Frame & gutter cleaning
  • Moss & algae removal
  • Interior & exterior glass

Gutter Clearing

Blocked gutters can cause serious damage including damp, structural problems, and roof damage. My gutter clearing service removes all leaves, debris, and blockages to keep water flowing freely and protect your property from water ingress.

  • Full debris removal
  • Downpipe checks
  • Prevents water damage
  • Before & after photos

Solar Panel Cleaning

Dirty solar panels can lose up to 30% of their energy output. My specialist cleaning service uses purified water to safely clean your panels without scratching or damaging the surface, restoring maximum efficiency and protecting your investment.

  • Purified water process
  • No scratch guarantee
  • Restores up to 30% output
  • All panel types
